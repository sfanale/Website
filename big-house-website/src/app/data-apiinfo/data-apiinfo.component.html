Historical Options Data by Fanale Research Group

My primary goal for this project is get historical options data into the hands of fellow Quant enthusiasts as cheaply as possible.
-Stephen Fanale, Founder

We proudly offer one of the most robust historical option data APIs available on the internet, at the most reasonable price possible. All of our technology was built for the cloud. This allows us to offer highly available, highly scalable services at the lowest possible cost. With our server-less api approach, we can get, store, and deliver data to you quickly and easily. This page will go into detail about the types of requests you can make from our API.

The first step in gaining access to this data is to set up an account and subscribe. You will get a confirmation email with instructions on making the most of our API offering. If you already have an account, log in and navigate to your Subscription Management.

Key Metrics

  • Over 6000 supported US tickers, including stocks and common ETFs
  • Over 10,000,000 daily close prices
  • Intraday prices for Top 100 tickers (15 minutes but higher frequency coming!)
  • Supporting information such as Open interest, volume, bid, ask, industry, sector, and implied volatility
  • Prices and extensive supporting information of underlying assets
  • Includes Fanale Research Premium membership
  • More features coming!

Endpoint:/options/?operation=read_list&operand1=ticker:strike:expiry
Inputs:
  • ticker(required) - underlying ticker
  • strike (optional) -Strike price to match
  • expiry - minimum expiration date
